Description:
Control a grumpy ghost by making the right noises! Ectoplasm is a game of moans; control Ecto the ghost by making the oooOOoooooOOoo noise all good ghosts make into your microphone! Be careful not to touch anything the -- more you haunt, the higher the score!

This game was made in 24 hours as a part of Playdemic's Ship-it-day by Mike & Laura!

ghost, mansion, haunted, microphone, control, touch
