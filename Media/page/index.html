Ectoplasm!
A 24 Hour Game by Mike Cann & L. Whyte!
Control a grumpy ghost by making the right noises! Ectoplasm is a game of moans -- move Ecto the ghost by making the oooOOoooooOOoo noise that all good ghosts make into your microphone! Be careful not to touch anything -- the more you haunt, the higher the score!

*Give permission to access your mic and/or camera to use voice input.

*Disable the mic by clicking on the icon in the bottom left corner (the game is volume based - loud music or other background noise will affect gameplay).

*Play by CLICKING THE MOUSE anywhere if you're in the library, a classroom, or office where oOOoooing might be frowned upon! Click and hold for extended lift!
